Admission Process

Simple steps to join our school family

Step 1

Fill the online application form with accurate details

Step 2

Submit required documents and application fee

Step 3

Attend entrance test and interview (if applicable)

Step 4

Receive admission confirmation and complete enrollment

Required Documents

  • Birth Certificate (Original & Copy)
  • Previous School Transfer Certificate
  • Academic Records / Report Cards
  • Aadhar Card (Student & Parents)
  • Passport Size Photographs (4 copies)
  • Address Proof (Electricity Bill/Ration Card)
  • Caste Certificate (if applicable)
  • Income Certificate (if applicable)
