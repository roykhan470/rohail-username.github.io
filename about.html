About KES

Learn more about our journey, mission, and commitment to excellence

Welcome to Kokan Educational Society

Since 1999, we have been dedicated to providing quality education and shaping future leaders.

Kokan Educational Society (KES) was established with a vision to provide world-class education to students in the Kokan region. Over the past 25 years, we have grown from a small school to a comprehensive educational institution serving thousands of students.

Our commitment to academic excellence, character development, and holistic growth has made us a trusted name in education. We believe in nurturing not just students, but future leaders who will make a positive impact on society.

Our Mission

To provide quality education that empowers students with knowledge, skills, and values to excel in life and contribute meaningfully to society.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution recognized for academic excellence, innovative teaching methodologies, and holistic student development.

Our Values

Integrity, Excellence, Respect, Innovation, Collaboration, and Compassion form the foundation of everything we do at KES.

Our Journey

Milestones that shaped our institution

1999 - Foundation

Kokan Educational Society was established with just 50 students and a vision to transform education in the region.

2005 - New Campus

Expanded to a new state-of-the-art campus with modern facilities including science labs, computer labs, and sports complex.

2010 - National Recognition

Received national recognition for academic excellence and innovative teaching methodologies.

2015 - Digital Transformation

Implemented smart classrooms and digital learning platforms to enhance the educational experience.

2020 - 5000+ Students

Reached a milestone of serving over 5000 students with 250+ dedicated faculty members.

2024 - Excellence Continues

Celebrating 25 years of educational excellence and preparing for an even brighter future.
